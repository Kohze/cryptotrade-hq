Setting it Up

To participate in the 7 days trading challenge, please create new bitfinex account or take a empty one and send 21 Miota to the account. Those 21 miota can be either in the exchange or margin wallet. In case you use a old account and have micro amounts left, please move them to the funding wallet (unless its less than 0.01$).

Competition

To compete and compare all trades, we require each participant to submit their API and Slack name to the registration form above. You find the API key in your bitfinex profile under "APIs", then generate one with all read permissions checked. The leaderboard will be activated once the competition starts.

Goal

The competition is all about learning to aggressively trade in the volatile crypto environments. The leaderboard and aim of the challenge is to maximize the amount of $ by the end of the 7 days. The winner will be added to the about page with the remark/symbol "Cryptotrade challenge winner".
